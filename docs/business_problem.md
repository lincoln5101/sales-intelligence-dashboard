# Business Problem

## Background

Apex Distribution Inc. is a fictional distributor selling office supplies, furniture, and tech products across the US. The company was pulling sales numbers from different spreadsheets with no single view of performance.

## The problem

Sales generates thousands of order lines every month, but leadership couldn't easily answer:

- Revenue, profit, and margin by region, segment, and category
- Which customers and products drive profit vs. just volume
- Whether any categories or regions are slipping
- Whether discounting was worth it

## Who cares

| Role | What they need |
|------|----------------|
| CEO / CFO | Revenue, profit, margin, trends |
| VP Sales | Regional performance, top customers |
| Product | Category and sub-category profitability |
| Operations | Shipping and order volume |

## What success looks like

1. Clean sales data in a relational database
2. SQL and a dashboard that answer the questions above
3. A few clear insights leadership can act on

## Questions the dashboard answers

1. Total revenue, profit, and margin for the period
2. Which regions and segments are growing or declining
3. Best and worst product categories by margin
4. How discounts affect profit
5. Top customers by revenue and profit
6. Monthly and quarterly trends
