# Business Problem

## Company Context

**Apex Distribution Inc.** is a fictional mid-size B2B/B2C distributor selling office supplies, furniture, and technology products across the United States. Leadership needs a single source of truth for sales performance instead of relying on scattered spreadsheets.

## The Problem

The sales team generates thousands of order line items monthly, but executives currently lack:

- A unified view of revenue, profit, and margin by region, segment, and product category
- Visibility into which customers and products drive profitability vs. volume
- Trend analysis to spot declining categories or underperforming regions
- Data-backed answers for pricing, discounting, and resource allocation decisions

## Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Need |
|-------------|------|
| CEO / CFO | Revenue, profit, margin KPIs; YoY trends |
| VP Sales | Regional and rep performance; customer concentration |
| Product Managers | Category and sub-category profitability |
| Operations | Shipping mode efficiency; order volume patterns |

## Success Criteria

A successful analytics solution will:

1. Consolidate raw sales data into a clean, queryable relational database
2. Answer executive questions via SQL and a Power BI dashboard
3. Surface at least 3 actionable business insights
4. Be reproducible — anyone can clone the repo and rebuild the pipeline

## Key Business Questions

1. What is total revenue, profit, and profit margin for the period?
2. Which regions and customer segments are growing or declining?
3. Which product categories have the highest and lowest margins?
4. How do discounts affect profitability?
5. Who are the top customers by revenue and profit?
6. What are monthly and quarterly sales trends?
